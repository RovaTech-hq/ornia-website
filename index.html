One Platform, Every Caregiver

More than a sensor.
A complete safety system.

Ornia pairs a feather-light wearable with a full software platform — apps, dashboards, and a real-time backend — so everyone caring for your baby sees the same truth, instantly.

Hardware

The Wearable Monitor

A feather-light, ankle-worn sensor that learns your baby's unique vital fingerprint from night one — hypoallergenic, certified for newborn skin, gentle enough to disappear.

Parent App

BLE & WiFi pairing, offline-first sync, instant push alerts — iOS and Android.

Web Dashboard

Live vitals, trend analytics, and device management in any browser.

Clinician Portal

Telemedicine-ready access for the doctors and hospitals in your care circle.

Geofencing

Custom safe-zones with instant boundary-exit alerts, wherever your family goes.

Smart Escalation

Severity-tiered alerts that climb the caregiver chain automatically.

Security First

JWT + OAuth + 2FA, end-to-end encryption, GDPR/NDPR-aligned consent.
